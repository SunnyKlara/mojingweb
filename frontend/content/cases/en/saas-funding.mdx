## The Challenge

200+ paying customers and ¥8 M ARR domestically. The client wanted to enter the US market and close an overseas funding round.

## Our Approach

- **Valuation reframing**: Benchmarked to overseas SaaS multiples
- **Investor outreach**: Demo Day + 1-on-1 with 30+ early-stage investors
- **Due diligence support**: Finance, legal, product — parallel tracks
- **Go-to-market**: US entity setup + compliance

## Outcomes

- Round size: **$6 M**
- Valuation multiple: uplifted from 3× ARR to **9× ARR**
- Deal cycle: **90 days** from intro to wire
