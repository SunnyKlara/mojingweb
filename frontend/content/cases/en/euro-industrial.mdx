## The Challenge

Near-saturated domestic share. The client wanted Europe but lacked local resources, compliance know-how and channel network.

## Our Approach

- **Market diagnosis**: Locked onto Germany, Italy, Poland as priority
- **Compliance**: Expedited CE certification + German subsidiary setup
- **Channel build-out**: 12 agents negotiated and signed in 45 days
- **Operational support**: Weekly joint operations review (first 90 days)

## Outcomes

- Agents onboarded: **12**
- Year-1 revenue: **¥14 M**
- Collection cycle: **45 days** (industry avg. 90)
