## Trend 1: Nearshoring & Friendshoring

Over the past 24 months, manufacturing capacity in Mexico, Vietnam and India has risen sharply — reflecting systematic hedging against **single-source risk**.

## Trend 2: Green Compliance (ESG)

The EU's CBAM (Carbon Border Adjustment Mechanism) and CSRD directive are rolling out. Undisclosed emissions will directly affect order eligibility.

## Trend 3: AI Orchestration

- Demand-forecast accuracy up 15–30%
- Anomaly pre-warning window shrunk from 48h to 4h
- Dynamic routing cuts 5–12% logistics cost

**Takeaway**: Building clean data pipelines matters more than buying any AI tool.
