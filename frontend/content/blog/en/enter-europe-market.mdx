## Why Europe?

Europe is one of the most purchasing-power-intensive single markets globally. Consumers strongly favor "quality + sustainability." For Chinese enterprises, Europe is both a **high-margin B2B market** and the best proving ground for **brand credibility**.

## Step 1: Localized Market Research

- Target-country GDP, industry growth rates, main competitors
- Distribution structure (direct vs. agent vs. e-commerce)
- Regulations & standards (CE, RoHS, GDPR, etc.)

## Step 2: Compliance & Access

> Compliance is not a cost — it's long-term competitive advantage.

Build a compliance workflow: tax representative, local entity (or EOR), Data Protection Officer (DPO).

## Step 3: Distribution Network

- Recruit 2–3 core agents for balance-of-power
- Align KPIs + incentives
- Quarterly Business Review rhythm
